Major Egyptian Deities

The ancient Egyptians worshipped hundreds of gods and goddesses. Here are some of the most important.

Ra

The sun god and creator deity, often depicted with a solar disk on his head.

Isis

The mother goddess, known for her magic and as the wife of Osiris and mother of Horus.

Anubis

The jackal-headed god of mummification and the afterlife.

Egyptian History

Ancient Egypt was one of the world's greatest civilizations, lasting for over 3,000 years.

Timeline of Ancient Egypt

  • Predynastic Period (c. 6000-3150 BCE): Early settlements along the Nile
  • Early Dynastic Period (3150-2686 BCE): Unification of Upper and Lower Egypt
  • Old Kingdom (2686-2181 BCE): Age of pyramid builders
  • Middle Kingdom (2055-1650 BCE): Renaissance of Egyptian culture
  • New Kingdom (1550-1069 BCE): Empire period with famous pharaohs like Tutankhamun and Ramses II
